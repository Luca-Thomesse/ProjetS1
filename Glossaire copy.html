Glossary

Feminism

- Action that aims at the equality between men and women, and the extension of women's role in society.

Visuel

- The image or representation of an object or idea to aid in understanding.

Machismo

- Ideology of the superiority of man.

Inspire

- To transmit something, usually a knowledge or an idea.

Firearm.

Performance

- Realization of a work in live, in front of the public.

Ecstatic

- Who is in ecstasy, in deep contemplation.

Anti-segregationist

- Who is hostile/opposed to segregation.

Retrospective

- Presentation, summary of something, a work, an artist etc.
